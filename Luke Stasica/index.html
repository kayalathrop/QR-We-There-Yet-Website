Luke Stasica

(he/him)

I am all over the place when it comes to design practices. I started with photo manipulation and illustration back in the day and I love doing logo & branding work, apparel graphics, and poster design but I've recently gotten into motion design and 3D modeling. I love it! My computer, not so much.



My Special Sausage Boy ('S' is for Sausage)

This special meaty boy came to me by way of an intro to 3D modeling workshop. I had been trying to learn 3D myself for a couple weeks and the workshop really helped me dig deeper and try some things I had never done before. Sausage is both the best and worst thing I've ever made. I love him dearly and I hope you do too.
