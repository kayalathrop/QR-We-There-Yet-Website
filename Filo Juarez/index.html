Filo Juarez

(he/him/his)

Hi, My name is Filo. Inspired by Mexican culture, the art nouveau movement (specifically Alfonse Mucha), and photography. I graduate spring 2022 and cannot wait to see what the world of design has for me.



Long Live Women

I am from Oaxaca, Mexico. Learning about my culture has made an immense impact on my design inspiration. I believe women have played a crucial role in the creation of Mexicos heritage, therefor I created a piece in my printmaking class titled "Long Live Women".
