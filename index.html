QR...We There Yet? is a PSU Art and Design artist spotlight pseudo-gallery to be located on the second floor of FMH. Our purpose is to promote and share your favorite, "best in show" piece of work with others in the PSU design community. This website acts as our digital collection of artist and designer spotlight profiles.

Without sacrificing the physical, let’s keep stepping into the digital!
